Databases - traditional components

This chapter describes connectors for the most popular and traditional databases. These connectors cover various needs, including: opening connections, reading and writing tables, committing transactions as a whole, as well as performing rollback for error handling. Over 40 RDBMS are supported. These components can be found in the Databases family in Palette of Talend Enterprise Studio.

Other types of database connectors, such as connectors for Appliance/DW databases and database management, are documented in Chapter 8, Databases - appliance/datawarehouse components and Chapter 9, Databases - other components.

For database version support information, see the appendix on supported database versions of Talend Enterprise Studio Installation Guide.
