Accessing Talend Open Studio

The Setup wizard helps you to install Talend Open Studio application. If you unzip it manually, then follow the installation instructions provided.

Read and accept the terms of the license agreement to continue.

A Talend Open Studio Registration window prompts you for your email address and location. This information is optional. Click Cancel, if you do not wish to be informed for future enhancements of Talend Open Studio.

If needed, check the box to enable HTTP Proxy parameters and fill in the fields with your proxy details. Make sure you filled in the email address field if you provide proxy details.

You can fill in or edit the registration information at any time, through Window > Preference > Talend > Install/Update.

WARNING-Be ensured that any personal information you may provide to Talend will never be transmitted to third parties nor used for another purpose than to inform you about Talend and Talend's products.

Talend Open Studio opens up with the Login window.
